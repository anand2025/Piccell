About Us

Welcome to the photography club

We are the Piccell Club, the photography and videography club at IIIT Ranchi. Our aim is to capture the beauty of life through our lenses and create a community of like-minded individuals who share the passion for visual storytelling. We welcome everyone who loves photography and videography to join us and explore the world of creativity. At the PicCell Club, we are passionate about capturing life's moments and bringing them to the world through the lens of our cameras. Our club is made up of a community of photographers and videographers who are dedicated to honing their craft and exploring the endless possibilities of visual storytelling. Whether we are taking candid shots of our fellow students on campus, capturing the natural beauty of our surroundings, or documenting the excitement of an event, we strive to capture the essence of each moment in a way that is authentic and captivating. Our club is not just about taking photos and videos, though. It's about building a community of like-minded individuals who share a love for creativity and self-expression. We believe that the best way to improve our skills is by collaborating, sharing ideas, and supporting one another. So if you are someone who loves photography and videography, or if you simply want to learn more about this art form, we welcome you to join us at the PicCell Club. Let's capture the beauty of the world together.
